steps:

- Boil water; add tofu; simmer 2 minutes.
- Lower heat; whisk miso with a little hot broth, then stir back in—do not boil hard.
- Drizzle egg in a circle; rest 10 seconds; gently stir into ribbons.
- Ladle into bowl; top with scallions.
