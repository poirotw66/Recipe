steps:

- Heavy skillet over high heat until lightly smoking; add oil; sear steak 2–3 minutes until deep brown on the first side.
- Flip; medium-high; add butter and spoon over the meat 2 minutes more.
- Rest steak 4 minutes; fry egg in pan drippings to set whites, runny yolk.
- Slice steak against the grain; plate with egg and broccoli; more pepper if needed.
