- Cut chicken into pieces, and marinate with garlic, salt, black pepper powder, and toasted sesame oil for 30 minutes.

- Heat toasted sesame oil in a clean pan. Sauté onion and scallion, add honey cajun sauce until thick. Turn off heat, coat chicken, and garnish with sesame seeds.
