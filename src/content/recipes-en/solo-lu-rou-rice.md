intro: Fried shallot crisps are the soul of the dish; reduce the braising liquid until thick so it clings to rice.
steps:
- Slice shallots thin; fry in a small pot until golden; set aside some fried shallots.
- In the same pot, stir-fry ground pork until color changes.
- Add soy sauce, rice wine, rock sugar, and water; braise on low 15 minutes.
- Reduce until thick; stir in some fried shallots.
- Rice in a bowl; top with pork and sauce; scatter fried shallots.
storage: Refrigerate up to 3 days; add a little water when reheating.
